Beatrice's Pick

Merilu Pizza Al Metro

Merilu Pizza Al Metro is a quaint little pizzeria in the heart of Hell's Kitchen. Serving a thin crust pizza with its recipe's origin from Merilu's home town of Torino, Italy, all of the food at Merilu's - from salads to pasta to tiramisu - is made with family pride and years of experience. Merilu's smile and inviting nature with guests will make you feel as though you're in mom's embrace - and you know you'll never leave mom's with an empty stomach! Pizza Al Metro is their signature Pizza and it is quite tasty, with their homemade, hand pulled fresh mozzarella. I fell in love! Their fresh ingredients is tasted in every bite. Their most popular are - Italian Vegetariana, Rustica & Lou's Spicy Buffalo Chicken... those are just a few of the many on the list! And people can create there own pizza al metro!!!

The Pizzeria is located at 791 9th Ave., which is located right around the corner from the library where we take our coding classes. What a way to spend an hour lunch break with a quick bite New York NY, 10019. Merilu's is very small but the service was outstanding.

Merilu's Pizzeria website is www.merilupizza.com.
